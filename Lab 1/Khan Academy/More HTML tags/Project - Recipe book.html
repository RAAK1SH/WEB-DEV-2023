Recipe #1 - Sandwich

  • Time: 8 min
  • Serves: Sanzhar
How to cook

Step 1: Gather ingredients: bread, sandwich fillings (such as meat, cheese, vegetables, spreads, condiments, etc.), knife, cutting board.

Step 2: Prepare the bread: Toast the bread if desired, or leave it as it is.

Step 3: Assemble the sandwich: Place the fillings of your choice on one slice of bread. Spread condiments and spreads evenly.

Step 4: Close the sandwich: Place the other slice of bread on top of the fillings.

Step 5: Cut the sandwich: Use a knife to cut the sandwich into halves, or quarters if desired.

Step 6: Serve: Enjoy your homemade sandwich immediately or wrap it in plastic wrap or foil for later.

Recipe #2 - Hamburger

  • Time: 15-20 mins
  • Serves: Sanzhar
How to cook

Step 1: Gather ingredients: ground beef, salt, pepper, buns, cheese (optional), toppings (such as lettuce, tomato, pickles, onions, mustard, ketchup, etc.).

Step 2: Form the patties: Divide the ground beef into 4 equal portions. Shape each portion into a patty, about 1/2 inch thick. Season with salt and pepper on both sides.

Step 3: Heat the grill or skillet: Heat the grill to medium-high heat, or heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.

Step 4: Cook the patties: Place the patties on the grill or in the skillet. Cook for 4-5 minutes on each side, or until the internal temperature reaches 160°F (71°C) for a well-done hamburger.

Step 5: Toast the buns: Place the buns on the grill or in the skillet for the last minute of cooking. Toast until lightly browned.

Step 6: Assemble the hamburgers: Place a cooked patty on each bun bottom. Top with cheese (if using), then with the toppings of your choice. Spread condiments on the bun tops.

Step 7: Serve: Put the top of the bun on the hamburger and enjoy!
